# Leaderboard Command

The `/leaderboard` command displays rankings of top players across various categories - see how you stack up against the competition!

## How to Use

```
/leaderboard
```

Or specify a category:

```
/leaderboard [category]
```

## Leaderboard Categories

## Competing for Ranks

### Short-term Competition (Streak Leaderboard)

The **streak leaderboard** is the most dynamic:

- Changes daily as streaks break and rebuild
- Achievable for newer players
- Requires consistency over raw time investment

**Strategy:**
1. Set multiple reminders
2. Never miss a claim window
3. Consider Auror for easier maintenance
4. Claim before bed

### Long-term Competition (Claims/Level)

**Claims** and **Level** leaderboards favor long-term players:

- Accumulate over months/years
- Steady progress > bursts of activity
- Consistency wins

**Strategy:**
1. Play daily
2. Complete all daily quests
3. Maximise each claim session
4. Never take long breaks

### Skill-based Competition (Collection)

The **collection leaderboard** combines:

- Strategy (using resources wisely)
- Luck (claiming rare cards)
- Efficiency (crafting vs claiming)

**Strategy:**
1. Use Duplicate Prevention intelligently
2. Craft missing cards systematically
3. Save Galleons for strategic purchases
4. Focus on one rarity at a time

## Tips for Climbing Leaderboards

!!! tip "Ranking Up"
    **General Strategies:**
    
    1. **Choose Your Category** - Focus on one leaderboard at a time
    2. **Consistency > Bursts** - Regular play beats sporadic hardcore sessions
    3. **Optimise Daily Routine** - Follow the [daily routine guide](../daily-routine.md)
    4. **Set Goals** - Aim for specific ranks (e.g., "Top 100", "Top 50")
    5. **Stay Motivated** - Track your progress week by week
    6. **Learn from Leaders** - See what top players are doing differently

### Streak Leaderboard Strategy

**Focus Points:**
- Set reminders every 6 hours (buffer time)
- Claim right before bed
- Buy Time Turners for emergencies
- Consider Auror subscription
- Never travel without planning claims

### Level Leaderboard Strategy

**Focus Points:**
- Complete all 4 daily quests every day
- Vote on all sites twice daily
- Craft and disenchant actively
- Participate in all events
- Use XP boosters during multiplier events

### Collection Leaderboard Strategy

**Focus Points:**
- Complete rarities one at a time (start with Common)
- Use Duplicate Prevention before every claim
- Craft all craftable cards (orange icons)
- Save Galleons for Milestone Crates
- Be patient - this takes months!

## Troubleshooting

### "I'm not on the leaderboard"

**Causes:**
- You might be ranked beyond the displayed range (outside top 50-100)
- You haven't accumulated enough stats yet
- The bot needs time to update

**Solution:** Keep playing! Everyone starts somewhere.

### "My rank went down"

**Cause:** Other players surpassed you

**Solution:** This is normal competition. Keep playing to climb back up!

### "Leaderboard not loading"

**Cause:** Temporary bot issue

**Solution:** Wait and try again in a few minutes

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How often do leaderboards update?**  
A: Real-time! Your stats update immediately after each action.

**Q: Can I see historical leaderboards?**  
A: Not currently, but this may be added in the future.

**Q: Do leaderboards ever reset?**  
A: Lifetime leaderboards never reset. Seasonal/monthly leaderboards reset at the end of their period.

**Q: What's the benefit of ranking high?**  
A: Recognition, potential rewards, and personal satisfaction!

**Q: Can I compete with friends?**  
A: Absolutely! See where you rank compared to your server mates.

**Q: Is there a limit to how high I can rank?**  
A: No limit! Keep improving and you can reach #1.

**Q: Do bots/automated accounts affect rankings?**  
A: Suspicious activity is monitored and removed. Legitimate players only!
