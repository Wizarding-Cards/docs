## Trophy System

### What Are Trophies?

Trophies are points earned from achievements:

- Each achievement awards 1-10 trophies
- Trophy count is cumulative (never decreases)
- Displayed prominently on your profile
- Used for leaderboard rankings

### Trophy Values

**Trophy Distribution:**
- **1 Trophy:** Very easy achievements (first-time actions)
- **2-3 Trophies:** Easy achievements (early milestones)
- **5 Trophies:** Medium achievements (dedication required)
- **7 Trophies:** Hard achievements (significant effort)
- **10 Trophies:** Very hard achievements (elite status)

## Achievement Strategies

### Easy Trophies First

Start with beginner achievements:

1. **First Steps** - Claim first card
2. **Voting Champion** - Vote once
3. **First Craft** - Craft one card
4. **Quest Starter** - Complete one quest

**Quick 5-10 trophies!**

### Focus on Streaks

Streak achievements are time-based:

- Start building your streak immediately
- Week Warrior (7 days) = 2 trophies
- Month Master (30 days) = 5 trophies
- Set reminders, never break!

### Complete Rarity Sets

Work through rarities systematically:

1. Common Collector (easiest)
2. Uncommon Curator
3. Rare Assembler
4. (Continue as you progress)

Each completion = trophies!

### Daily Quest Commitment

Quest achievements are achievable:

- Complete quests daily
- Quest Enthusiast = 3 trophies
- Weekly Quester = 5 trophies
- Builds over time

### Vote Regularly

Voting achievements accumulate:

- Vote on all sites daily
- Regular Voter (50 votes) comes fast
- Supports bot while earning trophies
- Win-win!

## Common Achievement Questions

**Q: Can achievements be lost?**  
A: No! Once unlocked, achievements are permanent.

**Q: Do achievements give Galleons or items?**  
A: No, achievements give trophies and recognition only.

**Q: Can I see other players' achievements?**  
A: Yes, their achievement count shows in their profile.

**Q: Are there secret achievements?**  
A: Yes! Some are hidden until unlocked.

**Q: Do achievements unlock special features?**  
A: Generally no, but some may unlock badges or cosmetics.

**Q: How many total achievements exist?**  
A: Check `/profile` â†’ **Accolades** for current total (grows over time!).

**Q: Do event achievements come back?**  
A: Usually! Wait for next Halloween/Christmas for seasonal achievements.
