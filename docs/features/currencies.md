# Currencies Guide

Wizarding Cards uses multiple currencies for different purposes. Understanding each currency and how to earn/spend them is key to progressing efficiently!

## <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/galleons.png" alt="Galleons" class="hp-emoji"> Galleons

**The Main Currency**

Galleons are the primary currency used throughout the game.

### How to Earn Galleons

| Method | Amount | Frequency |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| **Claiming Cards** | 50-200 (streak dependent) | Every 3 hours |
| **Voting** | 100 per site | Every 12 hours per site |
| **Daily Quests** | 500+ | Once per day (all 4 completed) |
| **Leveling Up** | Varies by level | Per level milestone |
| **Events** | Bonus amounts | During special events |

### How to Spend Galleons

**Shop Items** (`/shop`):
- Felix Felicis Potion: 1,000 <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/galleons.png" alt="Galleons" class="hp-emoji">
- Time Turner: 250 <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/galleons.png" alt="Galleons" class="hp-emoji">
- Duplicate Preventers: 300-1,200 <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/galleons.png" alt="Galleons" class="hp-emoji">
- Caches: 500-15,000 <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/galleons.png" alt="Galleons" class="hp-emoji">
- Boosters: 500-800 <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/galleons.png" alt="Galleons" class="hp-emoji">

[See full shop guide →](../commands/shop.md)

### Galleon Management Tips

!!! tip "Smart Spending"
    **Priorities:**
    
    1. **Emergency Fund** - Keep 500 <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/galleons.png" alt="Galleons" class="hp-emoji"> for Time Turners
    2. **Strategic Items** - Felix Felicis, Duplicate Preventers
    3. **Collection Boosters** - Caches when you have excess
    4. **Don't Hoard** - Galleons exist to be spent!

**Monthly Earning Potential:**
- Claiming (3x/day, streak 50): ~11,250 <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/galleons.png" alt="Galleons" class="hp-emoji">
- Voting (4 sites, 2x/day): ~24,000 <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/galleons.png" alt="Galleons" class="hp-emoji">
- Daily Quests (30 days): ~15,000 <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/galleons.png" alt="Galleons" class="hp-emoji">
- **Total: ~50,000 Galleons/month!**

|--------|--------|-------|

| **Trick or Treat Events** | Variable | Special Halloween activities |

### How to Spend Sweets

**Halloween Shop**:
- Pumpkin Juice: 500 <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/sweets.png" alt="Sweets" class="hp-emoji">
- Trick or Treat Bag: 1,000 <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/sweets.png" alt="Sweets" class="hp-emoji">
- Spooky Potion: 750 <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/sweets.png" alt="Sweets" class="hp-emoji">
- Halloween Cosmetics: Varies
- Limited Edition Items: Varies

### Sweets Strategy

!!! tip "Halloween Planning"
    **Maximise Your Sweets:**
    
    1. **Claim Frequently** - More claims = more Sweets
    2. **Complete Event Quests** - Bonus Sweets rewards
    3. **Save for Big Items** - Don't spend on small stuff early
    4. **Limited Time** - Sweets only earnable during October
    5. **Leftovers May Convert** - Check if unused Sweets convert after event

## <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/crackers.png" alt="Crackers" class="hp-emoji"> Wizard Crackers

**Christmas Event Currency**

Available only during Christmas season (December).

### How to Earn Wizard Crackers

| Method | Amount | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| **Claiming During Christmas** | Variable split | Part of your Galleon reward converts |
| **Christmas Quests** | Bonus amounts | Event-specific quests |
| **Holiday Events** | Variable | Special Christmas activities |
| **Advent Calendar** | Daily rewards | December 1-25 |

### How to Spend Wizard Crackers

**Christmas Shop**:
- Butterbeer: 500 <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/crackers.png" alt="Crackers" class="hp-emoji">
- Present Box: 1,000 <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/crackers.png" alt="Crackers" class="hp-emoji">
- Holiday Cheer Potion: 800 <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/crackers.png" alt="Crackers" class="hp-emoji">
- Christmas Cosmetics: Varies
- Festive Items: Varies

### Christmas Currency Split

During Christmas, your Galleon rewards are split:
- 60% becomes Wizard Crackers <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/crackers.png" alt="Crackers" class="hp-emoji">
- 40% becomes Lumps of Coal <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/coal.png" alt="Coal" class="hp-emoji">

**Example:**
```
Claim reward: 100 Galleons value
→ 60 Wizard Crackers
→ 40 Lumps of Coal
```

## <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/xp.png" alt="Xp" class="hp-emoji"> XP

**Experience Points (Not a Spendable Currency)**

XP isn't spent but determines your level.

### How to Earn XP

| Method | Amount | Frequency |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| **Claiming** | 25-50 | Per claim |
| **Crafting** | 25-200 (rarity dependent) | Per craft |
| **Disenchanting** | 10-100 (rarity dependent) | Per disenchant |
| **Voting** | 50 per site | Per vote |
| **Daily Quests** | 100+ | Completing all 4 |
| **Events** | Bonus XP | During events |

### XP and Leveling

XP accumulates to increase your level:

```
Level 1 → 2: 100 XP
Level 2 → 3: 150 XP
Level 10 → 11: 500 XP
Level 50 → 51: 2,500 XP
```

Requirements increase as you level up.

### Level Benefits

- Unlock shop items
- Status and recognition
- Leaderboard rankings
- Potential exclusive features

## <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/resource.png" alt="Resource" class="hp-emoji"> Resources

**Crafting Materials (Not Traditional Currency)**

Resources are used to craft specific cards.

### Resource Types

- <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/resource.png" alt="Resource" class="hp-emoji"> **Common Resources** - Craft Common cards
- <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/resource.png" alt="Resource" class="hp-emoji"> **Uncommon Resources** - Craft Uncommon cards
- <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/resource.png" alt="Resource" class="hp-emoji"> **Rare Resources** - Craft Rare cards
- <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/resource.png" alt="Resource" class="hp-emoji"> **Legendary Resources** - Craft Legendary cards
- <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/resource.png" alt="Resource" class="hp-emoji"> **Mythic Resources** - Craft Mythic cards

### How to Get Resources

**Disenchanting Cards:**
- Common card → 1 Common Resource
- Uncommon card → 2 Uncommon Resources
- Rare card → 3 Rare Resources
- Legendary card → 6 Legendary Resources
- Mythic card → 12 Mythic Resources

**Bonus for Variants:**
- Shiny cards: 1.5x resources
- Gold cards: 2x resources

### Using Resources

Resources are spent via `/craft`:
- Common card: 3 Common Resources
- Uncommon card: 5 Uncommon Resources
- Rare card: 8 Rare Resources
- Legendary card: 15 Legendary Resources
- Mythic card: 30 Mythic Resources

[Learn about crafting →](../commands/craft.md)

## Currency Conversion

### Direct Conversions

**No direct conversion between currencies exists**, but:

- Galleons → Shop Items → Collection progress
- Resources → Crafted Cards → More resources (disenchant)
- Event currencies → Event items → Temporary benefits

### Indirect Value

**Relative Values** (approximate):
- 1 Time Turner (250 <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/galleons.png" alt="Galleons" class="hp-emoji">) = 1 claim opportunity
- 1 Felix Felicis (1,000 <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/galleons.png" alt="Galleons" class="hp-emoji">) = ~20% better odds for 3 claims
- 1 Milestone Crate (15,000 <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/galleons.png" alt="Galleons" class="hp-emoji">) = 10 guaranteed rare+ cards

## Currency Storage

All currencies have **unlimited storage**:
- No maximum balance
- Never expire (except event currencies after events)
- Safe to accumulate

### Event Currency Expiry

!!! warning "Use Before Events End"
    **Event Currencies May Expire:**
    
    - Sweets: Use before Halloween ends
    - Wizard Crackers: Use before Christmas ends
    - Lumps of Coal: Use before Christmas ends
    - Check announcements for exact dates

Some games convert unused event currency - check support server for details!

## Currency Earning Comparison

**Best Galleon Sources:**
1. Voting (4 sites × 2 times = 800/day)
2. Daily quests (500+/day if all completed)
3. Claiming with high streak (150/claim × 3-4 = 450-600/day)

**Best XP Sources:**
1. Daily quests (100+/day)
2. Voting (50 × 8 sites/day = 400/day)
3. Claiming (30-50 × 3-4 = 90-200/day)
4. Crafting/Disenchanting (varies)

**Best Resource Sources:**
1. Disenchant duplicate cards
2. Focus on higher rarities (more resources)
3. Participate in events (resource rewards)

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I trade currencies with other players?**  
A: No, all currencies are account-bound.

**Q: Do Galleons expire?**  
A: No, Galleons never expire.

**Q: What happens to unused Sweets/Crackers/Coal after events?**  
A: Check announcements - they may convert to Galleons or carry to next year.

**Q: Can I buy Galleons with real money?**  
A: No, Galleons are earned through gameplay only. Real money can buy cosmetics via Discord Store.

**Q: What's the best way to earn Galleons?**  
A: Vote daily on all sites (800/day) + complete daily quests (500/day) + maintain high streak.

**Q: How many resources do I need to complete my collection?**  
A: Varies greatly, but expect to need hundreds of each resource type.

**Q: Can I convert resources between rarities?**  
A: No, each resource type is separate.
