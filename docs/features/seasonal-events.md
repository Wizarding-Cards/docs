## Event Currency Management

### Earning Rates

**Halloween:**
- Sweets = Same as Galleons (1:1 ratio)
- Typical claim: 100 <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/galleons.png" alt="Galleons" class="hp-emoji"> = 100 <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/sweets.png" alt="Sweets" class="hp-emoji">

**Christmas:**
- Wizard Crackers = 60% of Galleon value
- Lumps of Coal = 40% of Galleon value
- Typical claim: 100 <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/galleons.png" alt="Galleons" class="hp-emoji"> value ‚Üí 60 <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/crackers.png" alt="Crackers" class="hp-emoji"> + 40 <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/coal.png" alt="Coal" class="hp-emoji">

### Currency Conversion

**During Events:**
- Regular Galleons may or may not continue (varies by event)
- Event currencies awarded in addition OR instead
- Check event announcement for details

**After Events:**
- Unused Sweets: May convert to Galleons or carry to next year
- Unused Crackers/Coal: May convert or carry over
- Check support server for conversion rates

!!! warning "Use Before Event Ends"
    **Important:** Event shop closes when event ends! Spend your event currency before the deadline or risk losing it!

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do events happen every year?**  
A: Halloween and Christmas are annual. Others vary.

**Q: Can I participate in events on multiple servers?**  
A: Event progress is account-wide, works everywhere!

**Q: What happens to unused event currency?**  
A: May convert to Galleons or carry to next year - check announcements.

**Q: Are event items permanent?**  
A: Consumable items are used up. Badges and cosmetics are permanent.

**Q: Can I still earn regular Galleons during events?**  
A: Depends on the event. Some replace Galleons, others supplement.

**Q: Do streak bonuses apply to event currencies?**  
A: Usually yes! High streaks multiply event currency too.

**Q: Will there be new events?**  
A: Possibly! Follow support server for announcements.

**Q: Can I buy event currency?**  
A: No, event currencies are earned through gameplay only.
