# Auror (Premium Subscription)

**Auror** is the premium subscription for Wizarding Cards, offering quality-of-life improvements and exclusive features for dedicated players. Support the bot's development while enjoying enhanced gameplay!

## What is Auror?

Auror is a **monthly subscription** priced at **£1.49/month** (approximately $1.89 USD) that provides:

- Extended gameplay windows
- Reduced cooldowns
- Cosmetic customisation
- Exclusive badge
- Support for bot development

## Auror Benefits

### Extended Streak Window

**Regular Players:** 12-hour window between claims  
**Auror Players:** 24-hour window between claims

**What This Means:**
- 100% more time to maintain your streak
- Sleep through entire nights without worry
- Much easier streak maintenance
- Less stress, more flexibility

**Example:**
```
Regular: Claim at 10 PM → Must claim by 10 AM
Auror: Claim at 10 PM → Can claim until 10 PM next day
```

This single benefit makes high streaks FAR more achievable!

Over time, this adds up to significantly more cards!

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Does Auror give more Galleons or better drop rates?**  
A: No, Auror only provides quality-of-life improvements, not pay-to-win advantages. Drop rates and rewards are the same.

**Q: What happens to my streak if my subscription expires?**  
A: Your streak is preserved! You immediately revert to the 8-hour window, but your current streak number stays.

**Q: Can I gift Auror to a friend?**  
A: Discord subscriptions are account-specific. Check if Discord gift subscriptions are available.

**Q: Do I get refunds if I cancel?**  
A: Refund policy follows Discord's terms. Generally, subscriptions are non-refundable.

**Q: Does Auror give me any in-game items?**  
A: No items are included, but easier claims mean more natural item earning!

**Q: Can I use Auror benefits on multiple servers?**  
A: Yes! Auror benefits apply to your account everywhere the bot is present.

**Q: What happens if the bot shuts down?**  
A: Subscriptions would be cancelled and refunded per Discord policy.

**Q: Is there a free trial?**  
A: Not currently, but £1.49 is less than a coffee!

**Q: Do you offer family/group plans?**  
A: No, subscriptions are individual only.

**Q: Can I upgrade mid-month?**  
A: Yes! Subscribe anytime and benefits activate immediately.
