# Voting Benefits

Voting for Wizarding Cards on bot listing sites provides substantial benefits for both you and the bot's growth!

## Quick Benefits Overview

**Per Vote Rewards:**
- <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/galleons.png" alt="Galleons" class="hp-emoji"> 10 Galleons
- <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/xp.png" alt="Xp" class="hp-emoji"> 200 XP
- <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/quest.png" alt="Quest" class="hp-emoji"> Progress toward daily quests

**Cooldown:** 12 hours per site

**Available Sites:** 4+ bot listing sites

## Why Vote?

### Player Benefits

**Direct Rewards:**
- Easy Galleons (no gameplay required!)
- Steady XP for leveling
- Quest completion (Vote Collector quests)
- Can vote from anywhere (mobile-friendly)

**Earning Potential:**
- 4 sites × 2 votes/day = 80 Galleons/day
- 4 sites × 2 votes/day = 1,600 XP/day
- Monthly: ~2,400 Galleons + ~48,000 XP!

### Bot Benefits

**How Voting Helps:**
- Increases bot visibility on listing sites
- Attracts new players
- Grows the community
- Helps bot rank higher in searches
- Shows support to developers

## Quick Voting Guide

1. Use `/vote` in Discord
2. Click a voting site button
3. Log in to the site (if needed)
4. Click vote on the site
5. Return to Discord for automatic rewards!

## Voting Strategy

**Daily Routine:**
- **Morning:** Vote on all sites
- **Evening** (12 hours later): Vote on all sites again

**This maximizes your rewards!**

## Vote Reminders

Enable voting reminders:

1. `/profile` → **Settings**
2. Toggle **Voting Reminders** to <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/check.png" alt="Check" class="hp-emoji"> Enabled
3. Receive DMs when votes are available!

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How often can I vote?**  
A: Every 12 hours on each site independently.

**Q: Is voting required?**  
A: No, but it's the easiest way to earn Galleons!

**Q: Do votes help the bot?**  
A: Absolutely! More visibility = more players = better community.

**Q: Can I vote on mobile?**  
A: Yes! All voting sites work on mobile browsers.
