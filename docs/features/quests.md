# Daily Quests System

Daily quests are special challenges that reset every day at midnight GMT/BST, providing bonus rewards for completing all four!

## Quest Overview

**Quick Facts:**
- 4 new quests daily
- Reset at midnight GMT/BST
- Bonus rewards for completing all 4
- Types: Claim, Craft, Disenchant, Vote

## Quest Types

### Card Collector <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/card.png" alt="Card" class="hp-emoji">
Claim specific rarity cards

### Card Crafter <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/craft.png" alt="Craft" class="hp-emoji">
Craft specific rarity cards

### Resource Gatherer <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/disenchant.png" alt="Disenchant" class="hp-emoji">
Disenchant specific rarity cards

### Vote Collector <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/vote.png" alt="Vote" class="hp-emoji">
Vote on bot listing sites

## Rewards

**Completing All 4 Quests:**
- <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/galleons.png" alt="Galleons" class="hp-emoji"> 500+ Galleons
- <img src="https://wizarding.cards/assets/emojis/xp.png" alt="Xp" class="hp-emoji"> 100+ XP
- Multiplied during Vault Key events!

## Quest Access

```
/daily
```

Shows current quests and progress.

## Quick Strategy

1. **Check quests each morning**
2. **Vote immediately** (easiest quest)
3. **Plan your claims/crafts/disenchants** around quest requirements
4. **Complete before midnight GMT/BST** to get rewards
5. **Repeat daily** for consistent progress!
