# Streak System

The **streak system** is one of the most important mechanics in Wizarding Cards. Your streak represents consecutive claims without letting too much time pass between them, and provides massive benefits for dedicated players.

## What is a Streak?

A streak is the number of consecutive times you've claimed a card while staying within the time window.

**Example:**
```
Day 1: Claim at 8 AM ‚Üí Streak: 1
Day 1: Claim at 2 PM (6 hours later) ‚Üí Streak: 2
Day 1: Claim at 8 PM (6 hours later) ‚Üí Streak: 3
Day 2: Claim at 8 AM (12 hours later) ‚Üí Streak: 4
```

## Streak Requirements

### Time Windows

You must claim within these windows to maintain your streak:

| Player Type | Maximum Time Between Claims |
|-------------|----------------------------|
| **Regular Players** | 12 hours |
| **Auror (Premium)** | 24 hours |

!!! danger "Streak Reset"
    If you exceed the time window, your streak resets to 0 and you must start over!

### Claim Cooldown

Remember that claiming has a cooldown:

| Player Type | Claim Cooldown |
|-------------|---------------|
| **Regular Players** | 30 minutes |
| **Voters** | 20 minutes |
| **Auror (Premium)** | 10 minutes |

**This means:**
- Minimum 30 minutes between claims (20 if you vote, 10 for Aurors)
- Maximum 12 hours to maintain streak (24 for Aurors)
- Sweet spot: Claim every 30 minutes to 6 hours

## Building Your Streak

**Focus:** Establish the habit

**Tips:**
- Set reminders every 6 hours
- Claim morning, afternoon, evening
- Don't stress about perfect timing yet
- Expect to break your streak a few times while learning

**Milestones:**
- Streak 7: First full week!
- Streak 14: Two weeks of consistency
- Streak 21: Three weeks - forming a habit

## Streak Emergency Plans

### Traveling

**Before Trip:**
- Stock up on Time Turners (3-5)
- Check timezone differences
- Set adjusted alarms
- Plan claim times around activities

**During Trip:**
- Claim during layovers/downtime
- Use hotel/airport WiFi
- Have backup data plan
- Set conservative reminders

### Busy Days

**Meeting/Work:**
- Claim before entering meeting
- Use lunch breaks
- Bathroom breaks work too (quick!)
- 2-minute task, plan accordingly

**Social Events:**
- Claim before leaving home
- Step outside briefly if needed
- Most friends understand a 2-minute break
- Better than losing months of streaks

### Technical Issues

**Phone Dead:**
- Use friend's phone
- Find computer/tablet
- Public library/internet caf√©
- Better safe than sorry!

**Bot Down:**
- Check support server for status
- Usually back up quickly
- Streak protection may be enabled during outages
- Don't panic immediately

## Advanced Streak Strategies

### The "Claim Sandwich"

**Strategy:** Claim at start and end of your active day

**Example:**
```
8 AM: Morning claim
11 AM: Mid-morning claim
2 PM: Afternoon claim
8 PM: Evening claim before bed
```

Creates buffer window overnight!

### Timezone Exploitation

When traveling east:
- Days are shorter
- More claims fit in calendar day
- Use for streak building

When traveling west:
- Days are longer
- Extra time between claims
- Easier to maintain streak

### The "Weekend Warrior"

**Strategy:** Extra claims on weekends

**Weekday:** 3 claims/day
**Weekend:** 4-5 claims/day

Accelerates early streak building!

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Does my streak affect anything besides claims?**  
A: No, streaks only affect the `/claim` command rewards and rare card odds.

**Q: Can I buy streak protection?**  
A: Not directly, but Time Turners and Auror subscription help protect streaks.

**Q: What's the highest streak ever?**  
A: Check `/leaderboard streak` to see current leaders. Some exceed 1,000!

**Q: Do server outages reset streaks?**  
A: No! During bot downtime, streak protection is usually enabled automatically.

**Q: If I become Auror, does my streak reset?**  
A: No, your current streak is preserved and you immediately get the 12-hour window.

**Q: Can I see when my streak will expire?**  
A: Yes! Your `/profile` shows time since last claim.

**Q: Is there a maximum streak?**  
A: No theoretical limit, but benefits cap at certain thresholds (200+ for max bonuses).
